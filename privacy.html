Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

1. Information used

With the creator's authorization, TSW2 Bot may access public TikTok profile information and public video metadata, such as video title, publication time, cover image, and share link.

2. Purpose

This information is used only to detect new public creator uploads and post notifications in a configured Discord channel.

3. Tokens and security

Access and refresh tokens may be stored securely on the bot's server. Secrets are not intentionally shared publicly or sold to advertisers.

4. Retention

The bot may store the identifier of the most recently announced video to prevent duplicate notifications.

5. Revoking access

The authorized TikTok user can revoke the app's access from their TikTok account settings. Stored authorization data should then be removed when it is no longer needed.

6. Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through github.com/TSW2x.
